Institutional Development Programme, National Agriculture Higher Education Project

Innovation is the enlightening power of a successful enterprise. To nurture and optimize skills and business development in this direction, the Inovation and Incubation Centre (IIC) was established under IDP-NAHEP at the University Centre.

The university supported by the World bank under the IDP-NAHEP, has a very ambitious plan to move towards excellence in the development of innovations, skill development and entrepreneurship. The IIC strives to enable the University students to receive first-hand experience in entrepreneurship, promote innovation driven activities, mentoring and a wide range of support and development opportunities.

Role: Research Intern

Duration: 2 Months

Objective

To have a self-sufficient Innovation and Incubation Centre at the university to foster entrepreneurial culture at GBPUAT.




Methodology adopted

To accomplish the targets and objectives of the NAHEP Internship, organised and systematic approach of divide and conquer was adopted for maximum efficiency.
